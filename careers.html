Current Openings

 

Director of Sales and Marketing

Job Location: Smyrna, Tennessee (Middle Tennessee)
 

If you are a proven sales professional in the transportation and logistics industry, make a rewarding career move. Join Boatman Express, and take a sales leadership role in one of the fastest growing trucking companies in the Mid South. Boatman Express specializes in meeting Middle Tennessee’s freight expediting demands, and we are now hiring a well-qualified Director of Sales and Marketing.

 

Job Description

The Director of Sales and Marketing will report to the Boatman Express CEO, and will be accountable for sales through initiating an action plan to identify and pursue new customers and new business development opportunities, and ultimately, secure new business. The Director of Sales and Marketing will lead corporate sales efforts from our Smyrna, Tennessee headquarters, and will also oversee in-house and/or outsourced marketing, branding and public relations initiatives.

 

Primary Job Responsibilities:

  • Develop, implement and lead strategic sales plans and forecasts to achieve corporate objectives.
  • Develop and/or manage strategic marketing plans - in-house or outsourced.
  • Develop and manage sales and marketing operating budgets.
  • Plan and oversee in-house or outsourced advertising and promotional activities, including print, online, electronic media, and direct mail. Manage any outsourced vendor relationships.
  • Ensure effective control of marketing results, and take corrective action to guarantee that achievement of marketing objectives falls within designated budgets.
  • Oversee and evaluate market research and adjust marketing strategy to meet changing market and competitive conditions.
  • Monitor competitor services, sales and marketing activities.
  • Establish and maintain relationships with industry influencers and key strategic partners.
  • Guide preparation of marketing activity reports and present to executive management.
  • Establish and maintain a consistent corporate image throughout all service lines, promotional materials, and events.
  • Direct market channel development activity, and coordinate sales distribution by establishing sales territories, quotas, and goals.
  • Represent company at trade association meetings to promote services.
  • Analyze and control sales and marketing expenditures to conform to budgetary requirements.
  • Prepare periodic sales reports showing sales volume, potential sales, and areas of proposed client base expansion.
  • Review and analyze sales performances against programs, quotes and plans to determine effectiveness.
  • Perform additional responsibilities as assigned by CEO.
 

Required Credentials/Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in a business related field
  • Minimum of 5 years of experience in sales, preferably in the trucking or logistics industry
  • A track record of long-term success in sales and creating business relationships
  • Willingness and demonstrated ability to sell both Inside (phone and email solicitation) and Outside (face-to-face meetings, proposals, closings)
  • Willingness to travel, as needed
  • Ability to work independently - ideal candidate will be able to plan, schedule, and organize work priorities and objectives with minimal support or daily direction

Boatman Express is a leading freight expeditor located in Smyrna, Tennessee. We provide same-day pickup/delivery services throughout the South and Midwest 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. At Boatman Express, we expedite every kind and size of time-sensitive cargo, from one-box emergency deliveries to trailer-sized loads. Customers include small local businesses and large corporate clients such as Nissan.
